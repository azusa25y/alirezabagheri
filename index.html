Alireza Bagheri

Ph.D., Electrical Engineering

New Jersey Institute of Technology

Electrical and Computer Engineering @NJIT

Alireza Bagheri is a graduate student with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), Newark, NJ 07102, USA. He is pursuing his PhD under the guidance of Dr. Osvaldo Simeone at The Elisha Yegal Bar-Ness Center for Wireless Information Processing(CWiP), NJIT, University Heights, Newark, NJ.

He received his M.Sc. degree with first-class honor in Communication Systems Engineering from the ECE department at Semnan University, Semnan, Iran in 2013. Prior to that, He did his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the ECE department at the Karaj Islamic Azad University (KIAU), Karaj, Iran in 2009.



Research Interests

His research interests are in the areas of design and performance analysis of wireless networks and wireless communication systems. These include, but are not limited to:

  • Mobile Cloud Computing
  • Cognitive radio networks
  • Cooperative communications
  • Cellular networks
  • Experiments on embedded system platforms
